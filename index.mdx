NPC Builder is a platform for creating AI-powered non-playable characters (NPCs) that bring your games to life with dynamic, context-aware dialogues. Here you will find guides and references to help you integrate NPC Builder into your projects, use our APIs for interactive NPC conversations, and manage your game's context (characters, worlds, etc.) through the NPC Builder system.
